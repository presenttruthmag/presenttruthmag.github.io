This week our Sabbath Creation Video Series features a fascinating discussion of what really happened at the Scopes trial. The propaganda of the news media and the movie Inherit the Wind have distorted the facts of the 1925 Scopes trial so much that the theory of evolution has today become the only theory of origins that is taught in secular schools today. Our speaker, Dr. David Menton, uses clips of the film and also news paper accounts of the day to show just how badly the debate between Clarence Darrow and William Jennings Bryan was distorted in order to unfairly favor Darrow and the theory of evolution. You will be shocked when you see how the lies and distortions of Hollywood and the popular media have played a major role in turning modern society against the Genesis account of creation and the plan of salvation as taught by our Lord Jesus Christ.

Dr. David Menton earned his Ph.D. in cell biology from Brown University and has served as a biomedical research technician at the Mayo Clinic. He also is an associate professor of anatomy at Washington University School of Medicine. Due to his vast understanding of the arguments of Darwinism he is very able to refute those false evolutionary assumptions with well documented scientific data in support of the biblical cosmology and to defend the biblical plan of salvation through faith in the Lord Jesus Christ as Savior, Creator, Lawgiver, and Judge.
