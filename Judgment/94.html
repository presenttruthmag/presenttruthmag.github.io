The publishers of Spectrum and AToday are leading their Adventist readers down the garden path to a total denial of even the basic teachings of the Bible. I just finished reading Desmond Ford's book, Genesis Versus Darwin . He completely rejects the literal six day creation account of Genesis 1-11 and the chronology and historical events described therein, including the literal account of the Fall, the world-wide flood of Noah, the tower of Babel and the later account of Jonah as mere theistic allegory. However, his explanation of the imagined billion year old geologic column has been completely blown out of the water by the geological events surrounding the 1980 volcanic eruption of Mount Saint Helens. This event laid down a "geologic column" in a matter of a couple of days not millions of years.

I debated an anthropology professor at Loma Linda, Dr. Sterling, a few years ago who openly advocated progressive creationism and theistic evolution as "fact" and that Genesis must be taken as metaphor and allegory. These people all claim to defend "sola scriptura", but then claim that the reader must allow for an "interpretation" that allows for the so-called "ignorance" of "early Man". Thus, Adam and Eve are metaphor. The sinless / deathless Garden of Eden is a metaphor. The satanic Serpent is a metaphor. The Curses pronounced by God are mere metaphor. The 24 hour day of the six day creation is mere metaphor representing billions of years.
