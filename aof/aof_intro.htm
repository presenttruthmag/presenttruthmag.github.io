In view of the flood of divergent doctrines, beliefs, and philosophies within the religions of this world in these final days of Earth's history, it is hoped that this compilation of the central articles of the Christian Faith will be of assistance to all who are being lead of God to seek the Truth.
     
It is our earnest desire to enable all to sort out this sometimes bewildering array of conflicting teachings and come to a secure knowledge of the true gospel of Christ. The gospel message declares in each and every article of faith that without a doubt, "God was in Christ reconciling the world unto Himself."

May these articles of faith be the basis for a truly Evangelical and scripturally grounded Christian Church which will lighten the Earth with the saving message of the everlasting Gospel of Jesus Christ.
